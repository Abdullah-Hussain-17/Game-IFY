ABOUT STUMBLE GUYS



	Stumble Guys is a massively multiplayer party knockout game with up
        to 32 players online intention to struggle through levels round after
        round of escalating chaos, until one victor remains! If you fall,
        just start again and run. Join the endless running fun!.Trust me this
        is game is so fun & the levels are epic too,you can't say that this
        game is copied from fall guys but it's inspired by fall guys. The
        developement Stumble guys started in August 2020 and was finished and
        published by Kitka Games in Oct 2020.
