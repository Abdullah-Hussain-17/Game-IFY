ABOUT MIRRORS EDGE 



	MIRRORS EDGE is a feauturistic,action,adventure theme game
        developed by dice and published by  Electronic Arts 
         on 2008 for x-box and ps-3 and 2009 for windows
        In this game you have to escape from police by doing parkour
        they will chase you and if they catch you will lose
        and if you escape from them then you will win. 
        You have to find your own way to escape
        this game is in first-person perspective 
        this game is inspierd by chase scenes featured in Casino Royale and the Bourne films
        Mirror's Edge features hundreds of first-person animations to convey Faith's movement
         and interactions with the environment
        Although the game is powered by Unreal Engine 3
        Upon release, Mirror's Edge received generally favorable reviews.
         Critics praised its visuals and immersive first-person perspective
        but some criticized its trial and error gameplay and restrictive levels
        The game sold more than two million copies worldwide and 
        won Adventure Game of the Year at the 2009 Interactive Achievement Awards
